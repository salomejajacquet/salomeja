is the design project developed by Paris based designer Saloméja Jacquet (b. 1994, Paris). Between research and crafting, SLMJA involves making clothing, textiles and tangible seat-like systems, as well as final pieces made to order. It acts as a nexus of collaboration. SLMJA seeks to experiment with shape and surface using soft materials—making garments and objects in a same way, in a continuous collection and in relation to the body as the focal structure. Grounded in functionality, these textile objects maintain the energy of an organic process with their craft-like qualities.

BIOGRAPHY

Saloméja Jacquet was born in 1994 in Paris. She graduated in 2017 from HEAD (Geneva, CH) in fashion design and accessories, after a two year-degree in fashion design in France.
